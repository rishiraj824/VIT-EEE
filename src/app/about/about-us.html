ABOUT VIT

VIT University was established under Section 3 of the University Grants Commission (UGC) Act, 1956, and was founded in 1984 as a self-financing institution called the Vellore Engineering College. It is headed by its founder and Chancellor, Dr. G. Viswanathan, a former Parliamentarian and Minister in the Tamil Nadu Government. The Union Ministry of Human Resources Development conferred University status on Vellore Engineering College in 2001. The University in recognition of its service to India in offering world class education, was conferred an honorary doctorate by the West Virginia University, USA. It persistently seeks and adopts innovative methods to improve the quality of higher education and suit international standards.

VIT University is one of the top private Universities in India in terms of Engineering and Management courses, Research facilities and highest rate of placements. The University has more than 200 MoU’s in Europe, America, Asia, Australia and Africa. The campuses have a cosmopolitan atmosphere with students and learned teachers from all corners of the globe. VIT has vibrant campuses with nationally recognized fests like GRAVITAS and RIVIERA held every year. The green campuses, cultural activities, clubs and the magnificent library makes the students’ experience interesting. VIT University looks forward to meeting you.
