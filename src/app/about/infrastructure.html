INFRASTRUCTURE

VIT Vellore campus is spread across a vast area of 250 acres and the VIT Chennai with 170 acres. There are seven academic blocks in VIT which are extremely specialized in their own particular field, namely- MB, GDN, CDMM, SMV, CBMR, TT and SJT. Fully furnished and well-equipped labs decorate every building in the University with over 55 servers to manage all the data transmissions. Moreover, the university is further decorated with ample features that support all kinds of events - Auditoriums, plush open spaces, outdoor stages and much more.

The need of the University to provide students with exposure to national and international knowledge experts, through regular national and international conferences / symposia and workshops is the result of a wide choice of modern conference facilities, meticulously planned and incorporated into the campus. The auditoriums can accommodate more than 2000 students all together. The Anna Auditorium, being the largest accommodates 1800 students and has been built with centralized air conditioners , Wi-fi service and a spectacular stage along with green rooms and perfect amount of lighting.

The wide spacious classrooms are well designed to incorporate everything needed for a pleasant learning atmosphere. The classrooms contain a white board and a projector, allowing teachers to switch to different modes of teaching as and when required. This hi-tech equipment enables our faculty to conduct classes in a way that enable students to make use of every available resource from one point at the click of a button. These classrooms are also often used for seminars and events from time to time.

Canteen and Sports Facilities

VIT has three fully equipped multi cuisine canteens present at three strategic locations in the camps. Two canteens at the far ends of college, one each in G D Naidu Block and the Silver Jubilee Tower and the central one near the Technology Tower. All the three canteens are on contract basis with external caterers and provide quality food at student friendly prices. Hosting a wide array of cuisines and dishes, they cater to students’ home style food. The University makes sports an integral part of its curriculum. It has 18 badminton courts, 7 basketball courts, 7 volleyball courts, 5 tennis courts, 4 handball courts, 2 throw ball courts, 2 swimming pools each in ladies and men’s hostel and 2 Football, Athletics, Hockey and Cricket fields are also available. VIT has indoor badminton and squash courts and also air conditioned gyms for both ladies and men’s hostel. Due to this the University also holds different sports fests.
