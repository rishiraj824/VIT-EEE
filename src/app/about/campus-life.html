CAMPUS LIFE

Every effort has been made by the administration and other management boards to ensure that all students find VIT a very active, fun and resourceful place to spend their academic years. Through bringing clubs, chapters and college festivals, students are not only exposed to a competitive environment inside the university but also with reputed institutions across the country and the world. Gravitas and Riviera help the students showcase the talent acquired through the 12 teams of motor sports, flying and mars explorers and the 80+ clubs and chapters, not forgetting the sports activities.

VIT also creates entrepreneurs through the Startup ecosystem and sponsorship of students’ projects every year. This makes it the best University for aspiring students. The University has 23 hostels: 17 men’s hostel accommodating 13000 students and 6 women’s hostels with 4500 students. The hostels have dining halls and canteens for each block. Hostels have a 24-hour medical clinic and stand-by ambulance. Also a main fully equipped health centre is available in the campus. For the students well being a counseling division is available in the University. Other amenities provided include eateries, convenience shop, souvenir shops and transport facilities around the campus in the form of shuttle cabs.
