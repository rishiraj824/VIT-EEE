INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS

VIT University has an International Relations Office (IR) to attend to the needs of foreign and NRI students. The University has about 300 students from all over the globe: Africa, Asia, America and Europe. The IR Office assists all these students to settle down through an orientation as well as a cultural dinner in their first semester. A study buddy programme is also put in place, where the senior students help the incoming students with their academics.

Internationalization started with the admission of foreign students followed by international MoUs. Besides the internal work of the IR Office, it has managed to have 200 MoUs within a short period all over the globe. The facilitate internships and exchange programmes with partner Universities. The Office further helps in the 2+2 programme where students study 2 years in VIT and the other 2 years abroad.
