UNDERGRAD COURSES

The most highly sought after B.Tech degrees from VIT University are achieved by successfully getting through the VITEEE examination conducted every year. The students based on their merit are given a range of options to pursue their under graduation degree. The university currently offers 25 undergraduate programmes, and in addition to this has recently launched the B.Arch degree for students willing to pursue Architecture. The admission into the B.Arch degree is procured through a successful NATA score. Many research centres are also part of the schools, which promote inter-departmental collaboration and provide a platform for students to delve into new research projects.
