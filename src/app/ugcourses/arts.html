Eligibility
  • Candidates who have studied in Regular, Full time and Formal Education are alone eligible to apply.
  • Candidates should have secured an aggregate of 70% and above in their +2 examination to be eligible to apply.
  • The aggregate marks obtained in the subjects in +2 (or its equivalent) put together should be 60% for the following categories:
    • Candidates belonging to SC/ST
    • Candidates hailing from Jammu & Kashmir and the North Eastern states of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura.
  • Candidates belonging to SC/ ST category must fill in the proforma which will be available in the information brochure and it has to be attested by competent authority.
  • Candidates hailing from Jammu & Kashmir and the North Eastern states of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura should fill in the CERTIFICATE TO PROVE NATIVITY proforma which will be available in the information brochure and it has to be attested by competent authority.
  • In case the marks statement is not produced by the candidate at the time of admission, the decision of the Management regarding his/her eligibility and admission to the programme shall be final.
Age Limit : Candidates whose date of birth falls on or after 1st July 1997 are eligible to apply.
