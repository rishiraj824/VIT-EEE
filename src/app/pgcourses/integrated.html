Procedure
There will be no entrance examination for admissions.

Candidates will be called for an counselling in the order of merit based on:
  • The aggregate of total marks secured in the +2 / Higher Secondary Examination for UG / M.Sc. Biotechnology (5 year integrated) programmes.
  • The aggregate marks secured in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in the +2 / Higher Secondary Examination for M.Tech. (Software Engineering).
